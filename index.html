Who are we?

Journey to the Metaverse,
is a collection of 4444 Wukong’s that have entered the Metaverse from their homeland of flower fruit mountain, in the Journey to the West universe...

THE First generation genesis collection of 444 Wukong’s are already here in the Metaverse, while the remaining 4000 will join them at a later date.

Our vision

The Idea behind “Journey to the Metaverse” started with founder panda’s LOVE of the Chinese novel “Journey to the West”- a childhood favorite.

There wasn’t a project themed around a story from Chinese culture like this. So we decided to create one that would bring this story to life in the Metaverse, complete with a game and a pfp.

Our story

The original Sun Wukong is thrown into the metaverse after trying to sneak into the Jade Emperor’s realm via his heavenly gate.

As he is being pulled into the metaverse, he tries to use his clone creation technique to pull himself out but fails and creates 4444 clones of himself. While in the metaverse the Wukongs emit and produce $Aura tokens.

Roadmap

Phase 1

Our OG community will mint their own first genesis Wukong, with an initial supply of 444, and a later follow-up release of 4000 Wukongs.

Phase 2

Release of our mobile game, featuring a play to earn mode exclusive to holders. non holders will be able to enjoy the game freely and still earn prizes, but their experience will be limited.

Phase 3

The Legendary Auction for our Wukong’s will begin. 50% of the profits will go towards our DAO Vault and 50% will go towards the development of the JTTM project.

Phase 4

Staking will be enabled, and our Token $Aura will begin generating post mint. $Aura token can be used to play the peach game. The $Aura Token has no liquidity pair, but the team will work towards a liquidity pair and make sure that the $Aura token delivers endless value.

Phase 5

The development of the Peach Game will be completed by this phase. Peach Trees will be airdropped to all Wukong Holders, and following the release of the remaining 4000 collection. The peach tree game will begin.

BUILDING PHASE

Life in the Metaverse will begin after the prior phases are completed. this includes Pixel land & Wukong houses that will be purchased with $Aura for your Wukong, as well as their voxel counterparts. The land and house will have various utilities of their own.

Phase 6

the Beginning of the merging of the Wukong. The Ascension/Descension event will begin with Wukong holders being airdropped peaches to merge their Wukong clones, and create a whole new form. Holders may use the Peach and 300 $Aura Token to merge and burn their Wukongs.

Phase 7

A Voxel collection will be available early 2022 for all Wukong holders, as mentioned the VX is claimable via $Aura Token in the JTTM shop. We will develop an entirely free claim for the holders.
