WHITEPAPER

Through the mystical energies of Heaven and Earth, the light of the Sun and Moon, the Mischievous Sun Wukong was hatched from a rock upon a high-top Mountain of Flowers and Fruit. After bowing in the four cardinal directions, Wukong opens his eyes to unwillingly shoot out beams of light into the mighty heavens and into Jade Emperors palace, alerting his entourage of the Monkey Kings awakening.

Over a period of a thousand years, Wukong explores and adventures through the lands of Flower Fruit Mountain, up until being informed about the Peaches of Immortality located in the Jade Emperor’s Kingdom…

The Monkey King’s quest for Immortality begins and after a long journey, Wukong finds the Gates to the Jade Emperor’s Kingdom and proceeds to barge his way in. Upon his entry into the garden of the Immortal Peach Trees, Wukong manages to pocket a handful of peaches before a battle between himself and the Jade Emperor’s Entourage begins. Days of battle ends with Wukong being blasted with a bolt of energy by the Jade Warlord in which the Monkey King is sent through a mystical portal. Wukong uses his clone technique to attempt an escape but is unsuccessful and gets pulled through the portal and into the metaverse.

After arriving in the Metaverse, Wukong’s Fillet is missing and to his amazement Wukong has been cloned 4443 times.

This begins Wukong’s new chapter of Journey to the Metaverse.

Through the use of the Ethereum blockchain, JTTM is designed to be the ultimate NFT Play-to-Earn mobile gaming experience. Our goal is to ensure that both collectors and everyday players receive the most out of their time and progression in the space. The team is developing a game that involves strategy, team skills and an ongoing lore that will continue to grow as our community strengthens. JTTM will have exclusive earnings through level advancement, unique tradeable items and an integrated token system called $AURA for JTTM holders.

How can I merge my two Genesis Wukongs?

When the blockchain contract is live for merging, those holders who have been staking at least two of their Genesis Wukongs from the start of the staking contract will have accumulated the required $AURA to purchase a holy/cursed Peach which will be used to complete the merging ceremony.

1. You will burn 700 $AURA to purchase a Peach off the JTTM marketplace.

2. The peach will be completely random with a 50% chance of redeeming either a Holy or Cursed Peach.

3. Burning the Peach and two Standard Genesis Wukongs will perform the descension into the pits of Hell or accession into the Heavens, resulting in an all-new Merged Genesis Wukong.

4. Holy/Cursed peach + Standard Genesis Wukong x 2 = Merged Genesis Ascended or Descended Wukong

5. There is no difference in the yielding of $AURA between Ascended or Descended Wukong.

Peach Tree Utility

The utility of the Peach Tree is very simple. Every Wukong NFT (Both Genesis & Gen 2) will grant its holder a NFT peach sapling that will grow into the peach tree upon staking. Peach Trees have 5 rarities: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary. Each rarity will net a different number of peaches per week.

Common: 3–5 Uncommon: 4–7 Rare: 8–10 Epic: 10–15 Legendary: 16–25

By staking the Peach Tree, holders will be able to claim a custom resource called Peaches. Through the process of burning the claimed Peaches to be turned into $AURA, holders will have the chance of receiving the NFT of the next yet to be revealed Journey to the Metaverse character.

Peach Game Mechanics
Peach Game mechanics are simple and straightforward. Each Wukong Holder has the ability to snatch un-staked Peaches from other holders — however, the number will be randomized. The chances of your Peaches being snatched varies; the longer you hold your grown peaches, the lower the chance that your Peaches can be snatched.

The Journey To The Metaverse mobile game will be based off Wukong’s new adventure in the Metaverse. After entering the Metaverse, Sun Wukong loses his Golden Filet (headband). He then begins the journey to recover his Golden Filet, but during his odyssey he must battle his way through the Metaverse, going against many evil enemies with the help of his new friends.




Play-To-Earn
Play-to-Earn will require you to hold a Wukong NFT granting you all of the functions, abilities and benefits available in the game. Non-holders can play, but the Play-to-Earn mode will not be available. Through project-to-project integration, we plan on allowing other projects members to have Play-2-Earn features but that will be expanded upon at a later date.

Secondary Market
Items from the game can be collected and sold on the secondary market (OpenSea) as standalone NFTs. The game mechanics will be expanded upon in our game whitepaper which will be released in the middle of Q2.
