WHITEPAPER

JOURNEY TO THE METAVERSE, ARE A COLLECTION OF 4444 WUKONG’S THAT HAS ENTERED THE METAVERSE FROM HIS HOMELAND OF FLOWER FRUIT MOUNTAIN IN THE JOURNEY TO THE WEST UNIVERSE. FIRST GENERATION GENESIS COLLECTION OF 444 WUKONG’S ARE CURRENTLY ENTERING THE METAVERSE WHILE THE REMAINING IS ENTERING ON A LATER DATE.

THE IDEA BEHIND “JOURNEY TO THE METAVERSE” STARTED FROM THE LOVE OF THE CHINESE NOVEL JOURNEY TO THE WEST THAT THE OWNER PANDA WAS IN LOVE WITH DURING HIS CHILDHOOD. THERE WASN’T A PROJECT WHERE IT WAS ABOUT A STORY FROM CHINESE CULTURE LIKE THIS. SO WE HAD DECIDED TO CREATE A PROJECT THAT WOULD BRING THIS TO LIFE IN THE METAVERSE, WHERE THE PROJECT WILL BRING THIS TO LIFE WITH A GAME AND A PFP.

OFFICIAL MINT PRICE

4444 WUKONG’S; THE COLLECTION WILL START WITH A 444 FIRST WAVE MINT OF GENESIS WUKONG’S, THE REMAINING COLLECTION WILL BE 4000 INCLUDING 14 LEGENDRIES. 2 LEGENDARY WILL BE AIRDROPPED TO ACTIVE MEMBERS OF THE DISCORD, 3 LEGENDARY WILL BE GIVEAWAY IN EVENTS, 3 LEGENDARY WILL BE IN THE MINTING POOL, 6 LEGENDARY WILL BE AUCTIONED AND PROFITS WILL BE ADDED TO OUR DECENTRALIZED AUTONOMOUS ORGANIZATION (DAO)

GAME OUTLINE

THE JOURNEY TO THE METAVERSE MOBILE GAME WILL BE BASED OF WUKONG’S NEW ADVANTAGE IN THE METAVERSE, AFTER ENTERING THE METAVERSE SUN WUKONG LOSES HIS THE GOLDEN FILLET (GOLDEN HEADBAND). HE STARTS HIS NEW JOURNEY TO RECOVER THE GOLDEN FILLET. HE MUST BATTLE HIS WAY THROUGH THE METAVERSE GOING AGAINST MANY NEW ENEMY’S AND MAKING NEW FRIENDS.

PLAY TO EARN WILL REQUIRE YOU TO HOLD A WUKONG NFT. NON HOLDERS CAN PLAY, BUT THE PLAY TO EARN MODE WILL NOT BE AVAILABLE.

GAME MECHANICS

ALL WUKONG NFTS CAN BE USED AS GAME CHARACTERS, WITH THEIR OWN ABILITY STYLE AND ATTACKS. ITEMS FROM THE GAME CAN BE COLLECT AND SOLD ON THE SECONDARY MARKET OR SOLD BACK TO THE GAME TO EARN $AURA TOKEN. WITHIN THE MOBILE GAME THERE WILL BE A MINI ARCADE STYLE GAME WHERE MEMBERS CAN VERSE EACH OTHER ON A SKILL-BASED GAME. MEMBERS CAN LINK THEIR WALLETS AND GAMBLE EACH OTHER’S ETH, TOKENS AND EVEN NFTS! MEMBERS CAN OPT IN TO GAMBLE THEIR COLLECTION OF NFTS AND WINNER TAKES ALL LIMITED TO 2 NFT BET PER GAME. MORE INFO WILL COME OUT WHEN GAME IS RELEASED.

SECONDARY MARKET

A SECONDARY MARKET WILL BE CREATED TO LET MEMBERS TO SELL/BUY TREASURIES COLLECTED FROM THE GAME.

DECENTRALIZED AUTONOMOuS ORGANIZATION (DAO)

OUR PROJECTS FUTURE DROP, GAME AND EVENTS WILL BE MAINLY DRIVEN THROUGH THE CHOICES OF THE DECENTRALIZED AUTONOMOUS ORGANIZATION (DAO). WE WILL BE LOOKING FOR OUR MEMBERS TO HELP US DECIDE ON MAJOR EVENTS AND PLANS FOR THE PROJECT. THE COMMUNITY WILL BE GIVEN CHOICES ON HOW THE JTTM VAULT WILL BE UTILIZED. ONLY HOLDERS ARE ENTITLED TO JOIN THE DAO AND VOTE.
