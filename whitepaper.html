WHITEPAPER

JOURNEY TO THE METAVERSE is A COLLECTION OF 4444 WUKONG’S THAT HAve ENTERED THE METAVERSE FROM their HOMELAND OF FLOWER FRUIT MOUNTAIN, on THEir JOURNEY TO THE WEST UNIVERSE. The FIRST GENERATION GENESIS COLLECTION OF 444 WUKONG’S ARE CURRENTLY ENTERING THE METAVERSE WHILE THE REMAINING 4000 will follow them and enter on a later date.

THE IDEA BEHIND “JOURNEY TO THE METAVERSE” STARTED FROM THE LOVE OF THE CHINESE NOVEL “JOURNEY TO THE WEST” THAT our OWNER, PANDA, WAS IN LOVE WITH DURING HIS CHILDHOOD. THERE WASN’T A PROJECT themed around a STORY FROM CHINESE CULTURE LIKE THIS. SO WE DECIDED TO CREATE A PROJECT THAT WOULD BRING THIS TO LIFE IN THE METAVERSE, and will do so with our GAME AND A PFP nft collection!

THE WUKONG COLLECTION WILL START WITH A first wave mint of 444 GENESIS WUKONG’S, THE REMAINING COLLECTION WILL consist of 4000 additional nfts, to make a total collection of 4444. GAME OUTLINE

THE JOURNEY TO THE METAVERSE MOBILE GAME WILL BE BASED OF WUKONG’S NEW ADVANTAGE IN THE METAVERSE, AFTER ENTERING THE METAVERSE SUN WUKONG LOSES HIS THE GOLDEN FILLET (GOLDEN HEADBAND). HE STARTS HIS NEW JOURNEY TO RECOVER THE GOLDEN FILLET. HE MUST BATTLE HIS WAY THROUGH THE METAVERSE GOING AGAINST MANY NEW ENEMY’S AND MAKING NEW FRIENDS. PLAY TO EARN WILL REQUIRE YOU TO HOLD A WUKONG NFT. NON HOLDERS CAN PLAY, BUT THE PLAY TO EARN MODE WILL NOT BE AVAILABLE.

GAME MECHANICS

ALL WUKONG NFTS CAN BE USED AS GAME CHARACTERS, WITH THEIR OWN ABILITY STYLE AND ATTACKS. ITEMS FROM THE GAME CAN BE COLLECT AND SOLD ON THE SECONDARY MARKET OR SOLD BACK TO THE GAME TO EARN $AURA TOKEN. WITHIN THE MOBILE GAME THERE WILL BE A MINI ARCADE STYLE GAME WHERE MEMBERS CAN VERSE EACH OTHER ON A SKILL-BASED GAME. MEMBERS CAN LINK THEIR WALLETS AND GAMBLE EACH OTHER’S ETH, TOKENS AND EVEN NFTS! MEMBERS CAN OPT IN TO GAMBLE THEIR COLLECTION OF NFTS AND WINNER TAKES ALL LIMITED TO 2 NFT BET PER GAME. MORE INFO WILL COME OUT WHEN GAME IS RELEASED.

SECONDARY MARKET

A SECONDARY MARKET WILL BE CREATED TO LET MEMBERS TO SELL/BUY TREASURIES COLLECTED FROM THE GAME.
